Project Summary

TFI Live Re-Imagined is a redesign of Ireland's national public transport app. The project focuses on real-time bus tracking, seamless ticketing, and accessibility improvements. Using the SCAMPER design thinking method, our team proposed data-driven, user-centric features that enhance reliability, inclusivity, and sustainability across Ireland's transport network.

Problem We're Solving

The current TFI Live app struggles with static timetables that don't reflect delays, complicated multi-step ticket purchases, limited accessibility for older or visually impaired users, and a lack of personalization or engagement.

Our solution: A frictionless, smart, and inclusive travel experience.

Unique Selling Proposition: The only Irish public transport app combining real-time GPS tracking, one-tap ticketing, predictive alerts, accessibility-first design, and rewards-based sustainability tracking.

End-User Profile

Demographics

People of all ages, genders, incomes, and education levels who actively use public transport. The working class and students represent the largest market proportion, as they use public transport daily and benefit most from the app's features.

User Overview

Active public transport users who want real-time updates, optimal route suggestions, and stay informed about delays in their daily commutes.

Current Frustrations

Delayed buses, unreliable timetables, inconvenient scheduling, lack of real-time information, and poor accessibility features in existing solutions.

Use Behaviors

Regular daily usage to arrive on time consistently, reduce travel stress, and live more sustainably.

Technology Profile

Regularly uses apps like Google Maps, Revolut, Deliveroo, and other mobile-first services. Comfortable with digital payments and real-time tracking features.
